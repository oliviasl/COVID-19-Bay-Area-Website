About Us

We're the Mountain View High School Girls Who Code Officers for the 2020-2021: Trisha Gongalore, Olivia Lai, Katherine Wang, and Lela Corson. We know a lot of people who wanted to aid those affected by COVID-19, but didn’t know how to get involved. So, we decided to create this website in the hopes of helping them find opportunities in the area!
