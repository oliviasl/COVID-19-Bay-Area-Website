Quarantutors

Quarantutors is an organization of high school and college students across the world offering free online tutoring to K-12 students in need of academic support due to school closures caused by the recent COVID-19 outbreak. Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, Quarantutors is committed to serving its community and nation as a whole in this time of limited student access to teachers, peers, and tutors due to government-mandated self-isolation. Looking for high school and college tutors.

Red Cross Blood Donation

In coordination with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), the Red Cross is seeking people who are fully recovered from the new coronavirus to sign up to donate plasma to help current COVID-19 patients. Patients who have fully recovered following a COVID-19 diagnosis may have antibodies in their plasma that can help those with serious or immediately life-threatening COVID-19 infections.

Santa Clara Contact Tracing

Contact Tracing to identify people who have COVID-19 or may have been exposed to COVID-19. This work will interrupt chains of transmission, slowing the spread of the virus through Santa Clara County. Looking for volunteers over 18 years old.

Volunteers must:
  1. Be able to serve as a Disaster Service Worker with Santa Clara County for 32 to 40 hours per week for a minimum of 6 months, preferably 1 year
  2. Have access to high-speed internet and a computer
  3. Preferred: knowledge of health coaching and medical terminology (ideally health-related experience e.g. RN, LVN, medical assistant, nursing student, MD, medical student, paramedic, health coach or health educator, EMT)
  4. Have excellent customer service/interpersonal skills; strong typing and word processing skills; data entry skills
  5. Have strong written and verbal communications skills. Especially needed: individuals fluent in Spanish, Vietnamese, and Chinese
  6. Have excellent attention to detail
  7. Be able to work independently with minimal supervision

Quarantunes

QuaranTunes aims to create a community of musicians who share their skills and passions with aspiring musicians around the world. While our lessons are targeted at children ages 4-14, we also offer masterclasses taught by world-renowned professionals that are open to anyone. Our teachers are volunteers and all of our classes are free. However, we encourage a donation to our charitable cause in return for our services. We believe in the power of music to connect and bring joy to people around the world, especially in these hard times.
